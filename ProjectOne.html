Thailand's Capital City

Stories from a fellow traveler

How is the thought of a trip to Thailand? It must sound pretty good if you made it to this page. You have good taste in destinations, but do you have the whole story. I spent most of the last eleven years living in Thailand and I can tell you that while it's my favorite spot on Earth, it's also a mixed-bag that is likely half a world away from where you're sitting. A trip like this should begin with research. Perhaps your research will begin here.
